    IFRS 18 knowledge base

    Three income statement categories, one clearer map.

    These IFRS 18 profit or loss categories are separate from the operating, investing and financing categories used for cash flows under IAS 7.

    01 / OPERATING

    Operating

    The residual category. Income and expenses are operating when they do not meet the definitions of investing, financing, income taxes or discontinued operations.

    02 / INVESTING

    Investing

    Income and expenses from assets that generate returns individually and largely independently of the entity’s other resources, such as debt and equity investments, investment property, and cash and cash equivalents.

    Income and expenses from assets used to deliver goods or services to customers are operating instead.

    03 / FINANCING

    Financing

    For liabilities arising solely from raising finance, all related income and expenses are financing.

    For liabilities that do not arise solely from raising finance, such as lease liabilities and defined benefit obligations, only the interest component is financing.

    Mandatory subtotals

    • Operating profit or loss.
    • Profit or loss before financing and income taxes, subject to the specified exceptions reflected in the assessment.
